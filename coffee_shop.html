Secrets of Our Great Espresso

Espresso means "made at the spur of the moment". It is an adjective applied to food and drinks that are made at the moment of asking, and in Italy it is chiefly used to describe coffee. The inimitable Italian phrase poco ma buono means "small but good". At AROMA COFFEE, we believe that the poco ma buono that we serve is a remarkable and controlled shot of espresso that is difficult to match. Our skilled and experienced baristas ensure that every shot of espresso served is a perfect shot. It has a caramel and nutty taste, a sweet and intense flavour, with a hint of acidity that is noticeable to your taste buds.
